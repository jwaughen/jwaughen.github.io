Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons salted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Microwave butter for 30 seconds to just barely melt it. Mix butter with the white and brown suagrs until creamy. Add the vanilla and egg and beat on low speed until just combined - 10 to 15 seconds.
  3. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until crumbles form. Use hands to press the crumbles together into a dough. Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Bake for 9-11 minutes until pale and puffy. Do not overbake.
  5. Let them cool and enjoy!

Keto Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter, or nut butter of choice
  • 1 tablespoon milk of choice
  • 2/3 cup finely ground almond flour
  • 3 tablespoons powdered erythritol or powdered sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Gently heat nut butter until runny.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together dry ingredients very well.
  4. Stir in nut butter and milk into dry ingredients to form a dough.
  5. Roll dough into balls, then flatten into thick cookies with a fork creating a pattern on each dough ball.
  6. Bake on center rack for 10 minutes.
  7. Take out of oven when still undercooked. Let them cool completely. Cookies will firm up as they cool.

Reese's Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter (room temperature)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups peanut butter chips
  • 1 bag of snack sized Reese's Peanut Butter Cups

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees and line cookie sheets with parchment paper
  2. In a stand mixer combine butter, sugar, and brown sugar. Beat for 2 minutes. Scrape down the sides of the bowl as needed. Add in peanut butter and beat for 1 more minute. Add eggs and vanilla and gently combine.
  3. In a separate bowl add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk together.
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients. Just before it's totally combined, add in 1 1/4 cup of peanut butter chips. Stir until just barely combined.
  5. Make dough balls (about 1/4 cup) and place them on cookie sheets. Bake for 9-11 minutes (less time for smaller cookies). Be careful not to over bake because peanut butter cookies can turn out dry if you do.
  6. When you pull them out of the oven, give them 1 or 2 minutes to cool and then press in the extra peanut butter cups. Just push them into the cookies to make them look picture perfect! They'll melt a but once added!
